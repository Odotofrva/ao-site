Electronics

RT-ONE Microcontroller Board

The RT-ONE is RoboTechi’s entry-level developer board designed to make learning electronics simple, accessible, and exciting. Inspired by the ease of use of Arduino, RT-ONE gives beginners, students, and makers a powerful platform to explore circuits, sensors, coding, and hardware fundamentals without the complexity or cost of traditional development kits.
