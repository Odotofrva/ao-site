ROBOTECHI

ROBOTECHI is a pioneering company at the forefront of custom-built electronics tailored for the Internet of Things (IoT), cybersecurity, and the vibrant maker community. With a commitment to innovation and quality, ROBOTECHI specializes in developing bespoke electronic solutions that empower businesses and individuals to harness the full potential of technology.

OBIOSCARD v1

The OBIOSCARD v1 is a custom-designed PCB that integrates a range of features for interactive displays and notifications. At its core, the card is powered by the versatile ATMEGA328 microcontroller, providing robust processing capabilities for various applications.

Micropad 4 Key v1

The (MP-4Key) Micropad Shield is an innovative accessory designed to enhance productivity for makers, programmers, and tech enthusiasts. Featuring four customizable MX keyswitches, this compact shield seamlessly integrates with Arduino Uno boards, providing a versatile solution for improving workflow and efficiency.
