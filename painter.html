About P3 Painting Co

My name is Orion, and P3 Painting Co represents more than just painting walls — it represents learning a real, hands-on trade.

Coming from a creative and technical background, I wanted to build a skill with a low barrier to entry, one that rewards discipline, consistency, and craftsmanship. Painting checked all those boxes.

As technology and AI continue to reshape the job market, I believe trades will remain essential. Painting is a skill that can’t be automated easily — it requires a human eye, steady hands, and pride in the work.

One of my biggest motivations is passing down a tangible, valuable skill to my son — something he can rely on no matter how the world changes. P3 Painting Co is built on that mindset: learning, growing, and doing honest work that speaks for itself.
