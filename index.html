About

Who is OdotNYC!?

OdotNYC is the creative identity of a New York City–based builder who merges technology, engineering, art, and street culture into one evolving ecosystem. A self-taught full-stack developer and electronics enthusiast, OdotNYC designs digital platforms, embedded systems, and experimental applications while exploring the intersection of software, hardware, and creative expression.


His work spans web development, electrical engineering concepts, interactive media, augmented reality experiences, and independent product design. Whether building immersive digital environments, developing electronics projects, or designing streetwear concepts, OdotNYC approaches each project with the mindset of a builder and storyteller.

Music plays a major role in his creative process. The rhythm, culture, and energy of sound fuel his daily drive to create, innovate, and stay inspired. From late-night coding sessions to sketching new concepts, music acts as the constant background that powers his focus and creativity.

Beyond technology and design, OdotNYC is a father and community-minded creator who believes knowledge should be shared. Through projects, events, and educational initiatives, he works to inspire others—especially youth—to explore coding, electronics, and creative entrepreneurship.

Brand Awareness

The Brand: OdotNYC

OdotNYC was born the moment I arrived in New York and realized the truth: all bets were placed on me. Survival meant rebuilding from nothing — mentally, creatively, and financially — in the loudest city in the world.

The 3 stars represent the constellation "Orion's Belt" as well as OdotNYC. Being my first name actually being "Orion", the logo was simple. OdotNYC lives at the intersection of technology, fashion, and art.
