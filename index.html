About

Who is Odot!?

Odot is a devoted father driven by a deep passion for both art and technology. As a multidisciplinary creative, he works daily to design and build innovative products that seamlessly merge visual expression with technical functionality. His work lives at the intersection of imagination and engineering—where creativity fuels invention.


Beyond creation, Odot is intentionally shaping his path toward becoming a tech giant. He invests in himself through continuous learning, earning certifications in data networking, IoT solutions, and embedded systems, strengthening the technical backbone behind his ideas.

He is the founder of RoboTechi, a company dedicated to teaching practical insights into IoT, electronics, and emerging technologies, while also serving as a launchpad for entrepreneurial experimentation across multiple ventures. Balancing fatherhood, creativity, and disciplined technical growth, Odot represents a new generation of innovators—builders who don’t just dream of the future, but actively engineer it every day.

RoboTechi Co.

RoboTechi is a technology-driven company focused on designing and building solutions around embedded systems and modern electronics. Inspired by platforms like Adafruit, Arduino, and Raspberry Pi, RoboTechi creates tools, resources, and projects that empower makers, students, and innovators to learn, prototype, and bring ideas to life through hands-on hardware and open technology.
